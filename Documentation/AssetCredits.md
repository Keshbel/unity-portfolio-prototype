# Asset Credits

No copyrighted or pirated assets are included.

The committed playable scene uses Unity primitives, built-in UGUI resources,
and manually configured project materials only. No third-party visual or audio
assets are included in the repository content pass.

The local `Assets/External/` working folder may contain CC0 Quaternius source
packs during content evaluation. They are intentionally not committed because
the current scene does not depend on them and the complete packs would add
unused files. Any selected third-party asset added later must be copied into
the project-owned art folder and recorded here with its asset name, source,
license, and URL.

## Committed Content

- Unity primitive environment, pickup, generator, extraction-zone, and enemy
  meshes: created in-editor, no external asset license required.
- Simple project materials: created in-editor, no external asset license
  required.
- Audio: none. Legal lightweight audio remains future work.

Recommended legal sources:

- Kenney
- Quaternius
- OpenGameArt with license checks
- Unity Asset Store free assets with license checks
